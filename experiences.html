Experiences

Terroir

Italian Wine Journey

Discover Italy's ancient wine heritage. Visit the celebrated estates of Lazio and Tuscany, taste indigenous grapes like Cesanese and Montepulciano, and enjoy a private dinner paired with rare vintages in our stone-arched wine cellar.

Maritime

Sunset Cruise on the Tyrrhenian

Board our private luxury yacht for an evening sail along the Tyrrhenian coast. Champagne, canapés, and the golden Roman sunset painting the Mediterranean sky. A moment frozen in time.

Mindfulness

Cliffside Yoga

Private sessions on our panoramic rooftop pavilion. As the sun rises over the eternal city's domes and ancient ruins, flow through asanas guided by master instructors. Complete silence, save for the whisper of the morning breeze.
